THE ORIGINS OF GOLD
A metal with ethereal allure

200 million years after Earth was formed, two ethereal neutron stars experienced a massive collision. During this cataclysmic explosion the first atoms of gold were manufactured and hurled out into the Universe. The Earth was hit by billions and billions tonnes of asteroidal material bearing the golden particles, arduously breaking trough the multiple layers, leaving traces, until reaching the Earth’s core. The dispersed golden treasure kept resting for thousands and thousands years underneath the surface, waiting for being discovered by the human kind and becoming one of the most revered precious metals eventually.
